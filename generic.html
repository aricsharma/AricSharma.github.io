About Me

With a background in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and a specialization in Computational Intelligence from Manipal Institute of Technology, I bring a systems-oriented mindset to solving complex data and technology challenges. My academic journey has been shaped by a strong focus on machine learning, artificial intelligence, and algorithmic thinking, complemented by hands-on experience across domains like logistics, finance, and embedded systems.

At Schneider Electric, I worked as a Transport and Customs Solution Intern, where I analyzed logistics performance data to identify operational bottlenecks and improve delivery timelines. I built Tableau dashboards to visualize key transportation KPIs and designed a customer facing WhatsApp chatbot that streamlined shipment tracking for over three million annual shipments across India. This role gave me real world exposure to large scale data systems, automation, and stakeholder-facing solution design.

Prior to that, I interned at Motherson Samvardhana International Ltd., where I contributed to the assembly and testing of smart junction boxes. I implemented key safety mechanisms like overload protection and fail-safe switchgear, gaining a deeper understanding of industrial grade electronics and hardware validation processes.

My project work reflects a strong interest in developing intelligent, user focused tools. Notable projects include a stock analysis chatbot powered by OpenAI's GPT-3.5 model and Yahoo Finance API, an IPL score predictor using mulitlayer neural networks trained on historical match data, and a Wordle game simulator developed in Python with color coded feeedback. I also explore data visualization through platforms like Tableau and Streamlit to turn abstract datasets into intuitive visual narratives.

Beyond technical projects, I served as Social Media Manager for MIT Revels, where I led the digital content strategy and boosted engagement to over 250,000 users. I enjoy blending creativity with analytics, whether through content, code, or communication. Proficient in Python, SQL, Tableau, and ML frameworks like TensorFlow and scikit-learn, I strive to apply my skills toward building scalable, impactful technology solutions.
